# Source Evidence

Official Fairfax County Public Schools (FCPS) sources used for the Rachel Carson Middle School to Skyview High School curriculum planner. Verified July 18, 2026.

FCPS pages are copyrighted by the Fairfax County School Board. This file therefore does not duplicate entire pages. Each record links to the complete official page or PDF, identifies exactly what the planner uses, and preserves a short excerpt with the supporting words highlighted using `<mark>`.

## Interpretation Rules

- The linked official page or PDF controls if an excerpt or summary becomes outdated.
- A countywide FCPS catalog listing does not establish that Skyview will offer the course.
- Skyview grades 11 and 12 remain future planning until school-specific materials are published as those grades phase in.
- Counselor, school, college, placement, staffing, enrollment, and scheduling decisions remain outside this planner.
- Highlighted excerpts show the evidence actually used; surrounding page content may add qualifications and should be reviewed before changing course data.
